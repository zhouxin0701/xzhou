Biography

Xin Zhou is a second-year Ph.D. of Computer Science Department, State University of New York at Binghamton, advised by Prof. Lijun Yin. In 2022, he conducted his research under the supervision of Prof. Can Wang at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), where he studied in the Intelligent Bionics Center. He also did a research internship in Shenzhen Technology University, advised by Prof. Xiaojiang Peng. His research interest lies in computer vision, affective computing, and motion intent recognition.
